BlackJack

In Blackjack, the dealer and player are both dealt 2 cards.
The object of the game is to get as close to 21 as possible without exceeding it.
Cards are worth their numerical value while picture cards are worth 10.
Aces are worth 11 unless 21 is exceeded, then they are worth 1.
If the player has 5 cards and still remains under 21, they win.
The Hit button is used to add another card to your hand.
The Stand button compares how much your cards are worth against the dealer.
The New Deal button starts the next round.
Good Luck!
